Our Story

Founded in 2018, Chill Wave Lounge was born from a passion to create an oasis of relaxation and luxury in the heart of the city. Our founder, James Fisher, envisioned a space where people could escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life and indulge in premium services.

Today, we pride ourselves on delivering exceptional hospitality, creating memorable experiences, and maintaining an atmosphere of sophistication and comfort for all our guests.

Our mission is simple: to provide a sanctuary where relaxation, style, and premium service come together to create unforgettable moments.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to our most commonly asked questions. If you need further assistance, don't hesitate to contact us.

Yes, we maintain a smart casual dress code. While we don't require formal attire, we ask that guests avoid sportswear, beachwear, and flip-flops in the evening. For the VIP lounge, smart elegant attire is recommended.

While walk-ins are welcome, we highly recommend booking in advance, especially for weekends and special events. For the VIP lounge and private pool bookings, advance reservation is required. You can easily book through our website or by calling us.

Children are welcome during daytime hours until 8:00 PM when accompanied by adults. The pool has designated family hours from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. After 8:00 PM, our venue becomes adults-only (18+) to maintain a sophisticated evening atmosphere.

Yes, we offer private event bookings for celebrations, corporate events, and special occasions. We can customize our services to meet your specific needs, including catering, entertainment, and exclusive use of certain areas. Contact our events team for more information.

We accept all major credit and debit cards, mobile payments (Apple Pay, Google Pay), and cash. For VIP services and private events, we also offer the option of setting up a tab or making advance payments.
